Choose The Plan That's Right For You

Looking to choose the perfect plan for your school management needs? Dyna Quest offers three comprehensive packages to fit any budget and requirement.

Basic Package: Perfect for smaller schools and institutions that require essential management tools to keep things running smoothly. This package includes features such as student records, attendance tracking, and basic financial management. Price: R 3,899.99 per month.

Advanced Package: For medium-sized schools that require more advanced tools and features to manage their institution effectively. This package includes everything in the basic package, as well as more advanced financial management, grade management, and communication tools. Price: R 4,699.99 per month.

Premium Package: Our most comprehensive package, perfect for large schools and institutions that require advanced tools and features to manage their operations efficiently. This package includes everything in the advanced package, as well as premium support, custom reporting, and advanced security features. Price: R 5,699.99 per month.

And remember, if you choose to pay annually, you can save 10% on your subscription. So choose the plan that's right for you and start managing your school with ease today!
