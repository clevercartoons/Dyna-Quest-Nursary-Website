How does it work?

  • Setup:

    After signing up for Dyna Quest, you'll receive a welcome email with your login details and a link to access the software. The setup process is straightforward and easy to follow, with step-by-step instructions provided throughout the process. You'll need to input your school's information and set up user accounts for your staff, students, and parents.

  • Using the software:

    Dyna Quest is designed to be user-friendly, with an intuitive interface that makes it easy to navigate and use. The software is accessible from any device with an internet connection, so staff, students, and parents can access it from anywhere, anytime. Dyna Quest covers all aspects of school administration, including student enrollment, attendance tracking, grade management, and reporting, making it a comprehensive solution for schools of all sizes

  • Monthly subscription:

    Dyna Quest is a cloud-based software, which means it is available for a monthly subscription fee. The subscription fee is determined by the number of students enrolled at your school and the features you choose to include. Our pricing structure is flexible, allowing you to choose the features you need and pay only for what you use. There are no hidden fees or long-term commitments, making it an affordable option for schools of all sizes.
